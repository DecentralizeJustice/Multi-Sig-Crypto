Setup

You generate a mnemonic phrase and only give me the root public key needed to create your other addresses.

Private Key Creation

I generate two private keys for you and an account level bip44 compliant private key. This give you two possible ways to access your funds. All of these procedures are done on a air gapped computer.

Multisig Address Creation

I use the public hex keys to create your multisig addresses.

Shipping

I mail you your addressees and private keys. This means of delivery ensures that the private keys I create for you have never been on line, and wont be until you spend from your wallet.
